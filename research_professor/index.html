Biography

Eun Sang "Jesse" Cha received the Bachelor of Science degree in Mechanical Engineering from the University of California, Los Angeles (Los Angeles, CA, USA) in 2015, followed by the Doctor of Philosophy degree in Mechanical Engineering from the same institution in 2023. Subsequently appointed as a Keck Endowed Postdoctoral Scholar at the Keck Data Science Institute at Pepperdine University (Malibu, CA, USA). Since 2025, serving as a Research Professor at the Institute of Advanced Machinery, Design & Technology, Korea University. Research interests encompass machine learning, computer vision, robotics, and autonomous racing.
