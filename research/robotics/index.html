Robot Intelligence
Robots are no longer mere machines to repeat a certain task. Due to a huge outbreak of Artificial Intelligence (AI), the diversity of the tasks robots can execute is explosively broad nowadays. In our lab, we are conducting research about robot intelligence, which is connecting robots and AI. In the abstract, various types of input, such as images and sound, obtained from the robot are aggregated to interpret the status of the environment with appropriate action(s) to take afterward.
