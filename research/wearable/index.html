Wearable Technologies
Wearable sensors have become more accessible nowadays. Automatically, researchers seek ways to apply them in daily lives, which were impossible before the ease of accessibility. In our lab, we are conducting research on applications of wearable sensors. In the abstract, inputs from the wearable sensors are collected as data to determine the status within the given circumstance or to support the motion in the exoskeleton.
